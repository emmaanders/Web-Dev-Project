About Me

THIS IS WHO I AM

I grew up in Salt Lake City, Utah. I have six people in my family, one older brother, two older sisters, and my parents. Our family has joined in two nephews and one niece that range from nine months to three years old. I have attended Brigham Young University for all of my college education this far. I have spent the past eight years helping my parents manage the franchise that they are a part of. The franchise is called Hokulia. I have enjoyed learning how to manage and market as we grow our business.

My hobbies include singing, learning new skills, painting, cooking, and spending time with my family. I enjoy travelling to visit new places and learning about different cultures. I have great interest in fashion, design, and marketing.

My long term goals for my career include graduating from BYU with a degree in Marketing. After graduation, I would love to be able to work doing social media marketing and put my passions into action. I have the dream of creating my own business and using the marketing skills I learn to promote and develop my company.
